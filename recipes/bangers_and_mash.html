Bangers and Mash Recipe

Ingredients

  • 4 sausages (pork or beef)
  • 2 pounds potatoes, peeled and cubed
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • Gravy (optional)

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Place sausages on a baking sheet and bake for 20-25 minutes until browned.
  3. Meanwhile, boil potatoes in salted water until tender, about 15-20 minutes.
  4. Drain potatoes and mash with milk, butter, salt, and pepper.
  5. In a skillet, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add sliced onions and cook until caramelized.
  6. Serve sausages over mashed potatoes topped with caramelized onions and gravy if desired.

Enjoy your delicious bangers and mash!
