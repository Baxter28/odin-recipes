Cottage Pie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, diced
  • 2 cups beef broth
  • 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  • 4 cups mashed potatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a skillet, brown the ground beef with onions and carrots. Drain excess fat.
  3. Add beef broth and Worcestershire sauce. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Transfer meat mixture to a baking dish and top with mashed potatoes.
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.

Enjoy your delicious cottage pie!
